Support Our Initiative

Your contributions help provide the support we need to facilitate our programs. We are a maximum effort non profit, which means we strive to access the most opportunities that are available within our operating budgets. We source equipment such as laptops, projectors, servers, and other computer components at reduced costs from hardware lifecycle companies, and utilize resources made available by our sponsors to provide access to technologies in under served areas. Our primary focus is education, where we seek to address the needs for technology infrastructure and learning centers. The other main objectives build upon the foundation of our education initiatives, as our mentorship and entreprenuership programs will encourage the development and integration of technologies.

Blockchain Foundation Africa

Blockchain Foundation Africa is a non profit that promotes the advancement of technologies. Our mission is to facilitate education and entrepreneurship programs in developing regions to increase computer literacy for Africans; and access to opportunities beyond their loacal reach.
